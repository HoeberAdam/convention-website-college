Throughout the entirety of Minecon, there will be various cosplay competitions. These competitions will be judged, and winners can expect to win cool prizes! All ages are welcome to participate, but please keep your costumes appropriate. More details to come.

In the past, Minecon has welcomed many famous faces from Minecraft. You can still expect to see many of these famous faces during Minecon 2013. However, these famous “Minecraftians” won’t always show their faces. It’s best practice to treat anybody wearing a mask with respect, because you never know who might be behind it!
